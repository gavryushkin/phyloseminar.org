Today's speaker is Don Klinkenberg.
Dr. Klinkenberg works infectious disease dynamics at the population level.
He did his PhD at the Institute of Animal Science and Health in Lelystad, the Netherlands.
He then moved to the Faculty of Veterinary Medicine of Utrecht University, first as a postdoc and then as a Lecturer.
He is now at the Center of Infectious Disease Dynamics where his focus is on combining genetic and epidemiological data.
