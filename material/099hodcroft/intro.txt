Hello everyone, and welcome to phyloseminar.org.
The current theme is on the novel coronavirus, and this the first talk in a series of 3 talks on that topic.
Please use the YouTube live comment box to ask questions, or tweet @phyloseminar.

Today's speaker is Emma Hodcroft.
Emma did her PhD with Andrew Leigh Brown, finishing her thesis on virulence in HIV in May 2015.
She completed her first post-doc position with the PANGEA_HIV initiative, continuing in Andrew Leigh Brown's lab, where she devloped a realistic, stochastic agent-based model to simulate HIV epidemics in sub-Saharan Africa.

Emma is currently a postdoc working on nextstrain with Richard Neher at the Biozentrum University of Basel.

Welcome, Emma, and thank you for participating!
