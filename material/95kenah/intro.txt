Today's speaker is Eben Kenah.
Dr. Kenah works on Survival analysis, Epidemiologic methods, Stochastic processes, Phylogenetics and casual inference
He did his doctorate in epidemiology with a masters in Biostatistics at the Harvard School of Public Health, working with Marc Lipsitch, followed by a postdoc at the University of Washington.
He then took a faculty position at the University of Florida, where he rose in the ranks and then moved to the Ohio State University, where is is now Associate Professor.
