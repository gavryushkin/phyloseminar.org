attending a seminar

Seminars are held via Google+ Hangouts. In order to attend, you'll need to be logged in to a Google account with Google+ enabled. Once you're logged in, visit our Google+ page a few minutes before the scheduled start of the seminar. You'll see a post for the live seminar; click the link to join. (You can also follow us on Google+, in which case these posts will show up on your Google+ home page.)

If you'd rather not create a Google/Google+ account, don't worry! The seminar will automatically be posted to our YouTube channel when the seminar is over.
