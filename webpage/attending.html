attending a seminar

Seminars are livestreamed on YouTube. Simply visit our YouTube channel and click on the talk of interest to you. The livestream will begin once we start broadcasting.

If you missed the seminar, don't worry! The recorded seminar will automatically be posted to YouTube channel when the seminar is over.

To ask questions, use the Live Chat box to the right of the YouTube video.
