attending a seminar

Seminars are held via YouTube On Air. Simply visit our YouTube channel and click on the talk of interest to you. The live stream will begin once we start broadcasting.

If you missed the seminar, don't worry! The recorded seminar will automatically be posted to YouTube channel when the seminar is over.

To ask questions, use the #phyloseminar IRC channel or tweet @phyloseminar.
