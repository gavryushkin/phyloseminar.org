attending a seminar

Seminars are held via YouTube On Air. A few minutes before the scheduled start of the seminar, visit our Google+ page where you will see a post for the live seminar; click the link to join. (You can also follow us on Google+, in which case these posts will show up on your Google+ home page.)

If you missed the seminar, don't worry! The seminar will automatically be posted to our YouTube channel when the seminar is over.

To ask questions, use the #phyloseminar IRC channel or tweet @phyloseminar.
